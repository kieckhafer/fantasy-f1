### New this year

We realized that constructors did not contribute enough to the points in 2025. The top constructor ended up earning less points than the last place driver. We've added the following two rules to boost constructor points:

1. Constructors will receive points equal to the average amount of laps their drivers complete
1. Constructors will earn Championship Points in the same manner they do IRL (25 for a driver finishing 1st, 18 for 2nd, etc)

We've also added a few new driver rules
1. If your driver wins a race where wet tires were used, they earn 60 points
1. If your driver starts in the last half of the grid, and finishes in the top 5, they earn 40 points
1. If your driver starts on the front rwo (P1 or P2), and DNF's before half the race is finished, they lose 50 points
